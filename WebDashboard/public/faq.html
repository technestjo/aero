Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AeroTwin XR.

What are the system requirements for AeroTwin XR? +

AeroTwin XR requires a VR-ready PC with at least an NVIDIA GTX 1080 or equivalent, 16GB RAM, and a compatible VR headset (Meta Quest 3, HTC Vive, or Valve Index). Internet connection of at least 50 Mbps is recommended.

Can I use AeroTwin XR for professional training? +

Yes! AeroTwin XR is designed for professional aviation training. We offer enterprise licenses for flight schools, military organizations, and commercial airlines. Contact our sales team for custom solutions.

How is my training data stored and protected? +

All training data is encrypted and stored securely on MongoDB Atlas with enterprise-grade security. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties.

What aircraft are available in the simulator? +

Currently, we offer the F-18 Hornet, F-35 Lightning II, Boeing 787 Dreamliner, and various helicopters. We regularly add new aircraft based on user requests. Enterprise customers can request custom aircraft models.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose from our various subscription plans.

How often do you release updates? +

We release major updates quarterly and minor updates bi-weekly. All updates are free for active subscribers. Check our Updates page for the latest release notes.

Can I train with other pilots online? +

Absolutely! AeroTwin XR supports multiplayer training missions. You can fly with instructors and fellow trainees in shared virtual environments. Perfect for collaborative training exercises.

What support options are available? +

We offer 24/7 support through email, live chat, and phone. Premium subscribers get priority support with response times under 2 hours. Visit our Support page for contact details.
