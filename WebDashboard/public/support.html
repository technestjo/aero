Common Issues & Solutions

🔧 Hardware Compatibility

Ensure your VR headset meets minimum system requirements. Check our documentation for detailed specifications.

🌐 Connection Issues

If experiencing lag or disconnections, verify your internet speed is at least 50 Mbps for optimal performance.

🎮 Controller Problems

Recalibrate your controllers in the settings menu. Ensure batteries are fully charged.

📊 Data Sync

Training data syncs automatically. If not syncing, check your account permissions and internet connection.
